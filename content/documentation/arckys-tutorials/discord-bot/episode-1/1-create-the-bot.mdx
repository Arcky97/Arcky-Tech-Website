## Create the Bot

Go to: [Discord Developers Portal](https://discord.com/developers/applications) and login with your discord account
<br/>
- Click "New Application"
<ImageWithCaption src="/images/documentation/arcky-tutorials/discord-bot-tutorial/New Application.png" caption="New Application" align="left" size="large"/> 
- Give it a name e.g.: `MyBot`
<ImageWithCaption src="/images/documentation/arcky-tutorials/discord-bot-tutorial/Create New App.png" caption="Create New App" align="left" size="medium"/> 

- In the side menu click on "Bot" and scroll down to Intents section and enable all 3.
<ImageWithCaption src="/images/documentation/arcky-tutorials/discord-bot-tutorial/App Intents.png" caption="App Intents" align="left" size="large"/> 

> Intents control what events Discord allows your bot to receive.
