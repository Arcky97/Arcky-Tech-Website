## v1.0.0 - Initial Release 

### Features
- Simplified Setup makes the setup for *Vending Machine* events a lot easier, and to apply changes as well.
- Vending Classes allows you to define different types of Vending Machines with unique speeches and behaviors.
- Discount and Overcharges create dynamic pricing to apply to items based on the situation, vending machine or player.
- Selectable Stock by Badges automatically unlocks items based on the player's earned badges.
- Bonus and Stuck changes allow flexibility whether the player has a chance on gaining a bonus Item and if there's a chance 1 or more items can get stuck.
- Multi Buy allows multiple of a same item to be bought at once rather than 1 at a time.
