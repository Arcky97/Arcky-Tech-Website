## Basic Features

<Callout title="Simplified Setup" color="cyan-900">
  The Setup for *Vending Machine* events is a lot easier, and to apply changes as well. 
</Callout>

<Callout title="Vending Classes" color="sky-900">
  Define different types of Vending Machines with unique speeches and behaviors. 
</Callout>

<Callout title="Discounts and Overcharges" color="blue-900">
  Configure dynamic pricing rules to apply *discounts* or *overcharges* based on the situation, vending machine or player.
</Callout>

<Callout title="Selectable Stock by Badges" color="indigo-900">
  Unlock specific stock based on the player's earned badges.
</Callout>

<Callout title="Bonus and Stuck Changes" color="violet-900">
  Define whether or not, the player has a chance on gaining a Bonus Item. And whether or not, there's a chance 1 or more items can get stuck.
</Callout>

<Callout title="Multi Buy" color="purple-900">
  Allow the player to buy multiple of a same item at once while otherwise the player has to buy 1 at a time.
</Callout>
