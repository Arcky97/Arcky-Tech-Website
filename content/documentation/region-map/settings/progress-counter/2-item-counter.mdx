## Item Tracking

Use this Setting to enable or disable the Progress Counter Tracking of found and received Items.

<SetVer
  v211="ProgressCountItems = true"
  v201="PROGRESS_COUNT_ITEMS = true"
/>

### Possible Values

- `true` = Found and received Items are **<Gn>tracked</Gn>**.
- `false` = Found and received Items are **<Rd>not tracked</Rd>**.
