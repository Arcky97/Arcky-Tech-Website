## Species Tracking 

Use this Setting to enable or disable the Progress Counter Tracking of seen and caught Species.

<SetVer
  v211="ProgressCountSpecies = true"
  v201="PROGRESS_COUNT_POKEMON = true"
/>

### Possible Values 

- `true` = Seen and caught Species are **<Gn>tracked</Gn>**.
- `false` = Seen and caught Species are **<Rd>not tracked</Rd>**.
