## Wiki

On the *Top right* of the Berry Plant Editor page, you'll find a button labeled **Wiki**. 
Selecting this button will take you directly to the official Essentials Wiki page for Berry Plants: 
<br/>
[Defining a Berry Plant](https://essentialsengine.miraheze.org/wiki/Berry_planting) page on the Essentials Wiki.
<br/>
There, you can find detailed technical information about every paramter that a Berry Plant entry in the PBS file may contain.
