## Other Parameters

Any custom parameters present in your Ability PBS files that are not recognized by the editor will be grouped under the **Other Fields** section.
<br/>
These values are preserved exactly as they appear in the original file.  
When saving, the editor will write them back unchanged, ensuring that custom plugin-specific or engine-modified fields are never lost.
