## Wiki

On the **top right** of the Ability Editor page, you'll find a button labeled **Wiki**.  
Selecting this button will take you directly to the official Essentials Wiki page for Abilities:
<br/>
[Defining an Ability](https://essentialsengine.miraheze.org/wiki/Defining_an_Ability) 
<br/>
There, you can find detailed technical information about every parameter that an Ability entry in the PBS file may contain.
