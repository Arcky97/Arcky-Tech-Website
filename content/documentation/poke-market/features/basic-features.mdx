## Basic Features

<Callout title="Smoother Money Changes" color="cyan-900">
  Player money *increases and decreases* in a smoother, more gradual way.
</Callout>

<Callout title="Limited Stock System" color="sky-900">
  Items in the market can have limited quantities, encouraging strategic purchases.
</Callout>

<Callout title="Seller Classes" color="blue-900">
  Define different types of sellers with unique speeches and behaviors.
</Callout>

<Callout title="Item Categories" color="indigo-900">
  Organize items into predefined categories for easier navigation and filtering.
</Callout>

<Callout title="Custom Item Categories" color="violet-900">
  Create your own categories to better suit your server's economy or theme.
</Callout>

<Callout title="Selectable Stock by Badges" color="purple-900">
  Unlock specific stock based on a player's earned badges.
</Callout>

<Callout title="Custom Speeches" color="fuchsia-900">
  Define personalized dialogues for sellers to enhance immersion.
</Callout>
