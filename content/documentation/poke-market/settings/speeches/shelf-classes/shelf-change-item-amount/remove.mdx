## ShelfRemoveItemAmount

The ShelfRemoveItemAmount is what the shelf will say when the comfirmed quanity is 0 of the selected Item. 
<br/>
It might seem similar to ShelfDescreaseItemAmount but this allows you to have a different speech for when making the quantity 0.
<br/>
It is possible to include more than 1 speech if you want to.
<br/>
Defining `ShelfRemoveItemAmount` looks like this:
<br/>
```ruby
ShelfRemoveItemAmount: [
  "You removed {1} {2} from you basket. Your bill was decreased by {3}", 
  "{1} {2} were removed from your basket. Your bill has decrease by {3}"
]
```
- \{1\} = Quantity removed (for ex. 3).

- \{2\} = chosen Items's name (singular or plural) (for ex. "Potions").

- \{3\} = Total price of chosen item * quantity (for ex. $600).
