## ShelfItemAmountChange

The ShelfItemAmountChange is what the shelf will say when selecting an item that you already added a certain amount of in your basket.
<br/>
It is possible to include more than 1 speech if you want to.
<br/>
Defining `ShelfItemAmountChange` looks like this:
<br/>
```ruby
ShelfItemAmountChange: [
  "You currently have {2} {1} in your basket, change it to how many?"
]
```
- \{1\} = chosen Item's name (singular or plural) (for ex. Potions).

- \{2\} = Current quantity of chosen Item in the basket (for ex. 5).
