## ShelfItemAmountDecrease

The ShelfItemAmountDecrease is what the shelf will say when the comfirmed quanity is lower (not 0) than the previous quantity of the selected Item. 
<br/>
It is possible to include more than 1 speech if you want to.
<br/>
Defining `ShelfItemAmountDecrease` looks like this:
<br/>
```ruby
ShelfItemAmountDecrease: [
  "You took {1} {2} out of your basket. Your bill was decreased by {3}."]
```
- \{1\} = Quantity removed (for ex. 2).

- \{2\} = chosen Items's name (singular or plural) (for ex. "Potions").

- \{3\} = Total price of chosen item * quantity (for ex. $400).
