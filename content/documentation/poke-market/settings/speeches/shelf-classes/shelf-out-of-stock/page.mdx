<header>
  # ShelfOutOfStock
  <br/>
  How to use and define Various ShelfOutOfStock speeches.

  ---
</header>

The ShelfOutOfStock is what the shelf will say when selecting an item that is out of stock 
<br/>
This only happens after buying all of them in stock and after checking out. 
<br/>
It is possible to include more than 1 speech if you want to.
<br/>
Defining `ShelfOutOfStock` looks like this:
<br/>
```ruby
ShelfOutOfStock: [
  "{1} are currently out of stock, come back {2}."
]
```
- \{1\} = chosen Items's name (singular or plural) (for ex. "Potions").

- \{2\} = time left until the Item gets restocked.
