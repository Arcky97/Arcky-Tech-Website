<Header title="ShelfOutOfStock">
  How to use and define Various ShelfOutOfStock speeches.
</Header>

The `ShelfOutOfStock` determines what the player will be prompted with when they select an Item that is **Out of Stock**.

<SHSpeeches speech="ShelfOutOfStock"/>

To define the `ShelfOutOfStock`, use this structure:

```ruby
ShelfOutOfStock: [
  "{1} are currently out of stock, come back {2}."
]
```
### Placeholder Reference 
- `{1}` = The chosen Item's name (plural) — e.g., `"Potions"`
- `{2}` = Time remaining until the Stock will be resetted — e.g., `"tomorrow"`
