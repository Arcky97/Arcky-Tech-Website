<Header title="NotEnoughMoneyItem">
  How to use and define NotEnoughMoneyItem speeches.
</Header>

The `NotEnoughMoneyItem` determines what the player will be prompted with when they don't have enough money remaining to add a higher amount of the chosen Item to their basket. This is only the case when at least 1 of this Item was added to the basket.

<SHSpeeches speech="NotEnoughMoneyItem"/>

To define the `NotEnoughMoneyItem`, use this structure:
<br/>
```ruby
NotEnoughMoneyItem: [
  "You are out of {1}. Change the quantity of {2} you have in your basket?"
]
```
### Placeholder Reference
- `{1}` = The Currency used by the Shelf event — e.g., `"Money"`
- `{2}` = Chosen Item's name (plural) — e.g., `"Super Potions"`
