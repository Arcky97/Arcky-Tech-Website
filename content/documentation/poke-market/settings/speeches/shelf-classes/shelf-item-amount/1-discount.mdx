## ShelfItemAmountDiscount

The `ShelfItemAmountDiscount` determines what the player will be prompted with when selecting an Item and there's a Discount applied to the Shelf event. 

<SHSpeeches speech="ShelfItemAmountDiscount"/>

To define the `ShelfItemAmountDiscount`, use this structure:
<br/>
```ruby
ShelfItemAmountDiscount: [
  "There's a discount {1} on {2}, how many would you like?"
]
```
### Placeholder Reference
- `{1}` = The quantity of discount (in percentage) — e.g., `"11%"`
- `{2}` = The chosen Item's name (plural) — e.g., `"Potions"`
- `{3}` = Discount price of the Item — e.g., `"$180"`
- `{4}` = Original price of the Item — e.g., `"$200"`
