<Header title="IntroShelf">
  How to use and define IntroShelf speeches.
</Header>

The `IntroShelf` determines what the player will be prompted with when interacting with a Shelf event.

<SHSpeeches speech="IntroShelf"/>

To define the `IntroShelf`, use this structure:

```ruby
IntroShelf: [
  "Is there anything catching your eye?", 
  "How nice, you can buy items from the shelf now too! :P"
]
```
