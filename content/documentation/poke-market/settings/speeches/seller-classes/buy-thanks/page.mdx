<Header title="BuyThanks">
  How to use and define BuyThanks speeches.
</Header>

The `BuyThanks` determines what the NPC will say when the player has bought their chosen Item(s).

<PMSpeeches speech="BuyThanks"/>

To define the `BuyThanks`, use this structure:
<br/>
```ruby
BuyThanks: [
  "Here you are! Thank you!"
]
```
