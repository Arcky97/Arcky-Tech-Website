## BuyItemMult

The `BuyItemMult` determines what the NPC says when the player has confirmed the chosen amount of the item (2 or more) they want to buy.

<PMSpeeches speech="BuyItemMult"/>

To define the `BuyItemMult`, use this structure:
<br/>
```ruby
BuyItemMult: [
  "So you want {1} {2}?\nThey'll be ${3}. All right?"
]
```
### Placeholder Reference
- `{1}` = Chosen Item's amount (**more than 1**) — e.g., `"2"`
- `{2}` = Chosen Item's name (plural) — e.g., `"Great Balls"`
- `{3}` = Final Item price (after discount/overcharge if any) — e.g., `"$1200"`
