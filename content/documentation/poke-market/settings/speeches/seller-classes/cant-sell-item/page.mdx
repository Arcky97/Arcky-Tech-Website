<Header title="CatSellItem">
  How to use and define CantSellItem speeches.
</Header>

The `CantSellItem` determines what the NPC will say when the player has chosen an Item that can't be sold.

<PMSpeeches speech="CantSellItem"/>

To define the `CantSellItem`, use this structure:

```ruby
CantSellItem: [
  "Oh, no. I can't buy {1}."
]
```
