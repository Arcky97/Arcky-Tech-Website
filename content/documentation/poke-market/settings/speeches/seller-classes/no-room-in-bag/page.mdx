<Header title="NoRoomInBag">
  How to use and define NoRoomInBag speeches.
</Header>

The `NoRoomInBag` determines what the NPC will say when the player has no more room in their Bag.

<PMSpeeches speech="NoRoomInBag"/>

To define the `NoRoomInBag`, use this structure:
<br/>
```ruby
NoRoomInBag: [
  "You have no room in your Bag."
]
```
