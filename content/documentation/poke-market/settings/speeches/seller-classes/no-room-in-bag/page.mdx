<header>
  # NoRoomInBag
  <br/>
  How to use and define NoRoomInBag speeches.

  ---
</header>

The NoRoomInBag is what the NPC will say when you would have no more room in your Bag.
<br/>
It is possible to include more than 1 speech if you want to but you can't add a Time or Day.
<br/>
Defining `NoRoomInBag` looks like this:
<br/>
```ruby
NoRoomInBag: ["You have no room in your Bag."]
```
