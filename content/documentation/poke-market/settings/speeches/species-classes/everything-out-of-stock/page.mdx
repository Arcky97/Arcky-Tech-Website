<Header title="EverythingOutOfStock">
  How to use and define EverythingOutOfStock speeches.
</Header>

The `EverythingOutOfStock` determines what the NPC says when the player has bought all Species for a Species event. (There's no restocking). The NPC will not say anything else after this speech.

<SPSpeeches speech="EverythingOutOfStock"/>

To define the `EverythingOutOfStock`, use this structure:

```ruby
EverythingOutOfStock: [
  "Amazing, you bought all my exclusive offers, I'm really rich now thanks to you!"
]
```
