<Header title="OutroSpecies">
  How to use and define OutroSpecies speeches.
</Header>

The `OutroSpecies` determines what the NPC says when the player exits out of the Species event.

<SPSpeeches speech="OutroSpecies"/>

To define the `OutroSpecies`, use this structure:

```ruby
OutroSpecies: [
  "Come again and I might have more exclusive offers for you."
]
```
