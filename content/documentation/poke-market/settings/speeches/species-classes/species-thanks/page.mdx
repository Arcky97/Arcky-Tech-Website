<Header title="SpeciesThanks">
  How to use and define SpeciesThanks speeches.
</Header>

The `SpeciesThanks` determines what the NPC says when the player has bought a Species.

<SPSpeeches speech="SpeciesThanks"/>

To define the `SpeciesThanks`, use this structure:
<br/>
```ruby
SpeciesThanks: [
  "Thank you for your purchase, we hope you'll take good care for {1}."
]
```
